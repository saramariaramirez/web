What is cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is a type of bullying. It all happens online, in electronical devices.
Victims are harassed, threatened, embarassed or targeted.
It can happen in social media, video games, web pages, chats... Cyberbullying hurts people.

What to do in case you are cyberbyllied?

If you are a victim of cyberbullying:
- Collect evidence
- Block and report the bully
- Protect your passwords and accounts
- Take a look to your privacy controls
- Tell an adult if you are a minor
- Never respond

How to prevent it?

Prevention is better than having to learn after the mistake or problem. Prevent cyberbullying like this:
- Educate yourself, your family and friends.
- Remember the digital fingerprint.
- Take care of yourself! Change your passwords.
- Less is more! Don't share everything
- Think before posting
- Be intelligent!
